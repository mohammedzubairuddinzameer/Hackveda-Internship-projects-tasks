# 📈 What is a Trend Line?

A trend line is a straight line drawn on a price chart that connects multiple price points to show the overall direction of the market.

Trend lines are commonly used to:

* Identify market trends
* Predict future price movement
* Detect support and resistance
* Confirm market momentum
* Assist in trading decisions

---

# 📊 Types of Trend Lines

## 📈 Uptrend Line

An uptrend line is drawn by connecting higher lows.

Characteristics:

* Rising prices
* Strong buying pressure
* Bullish market sentiment
* Higher highs and higher lows

---

## 📉 Downtrend Line

A downtrend line is drawn by connecting lower highs.

Characteristics:

* Falling prices
* Strong selling pressure
* Bearish market sentiment
* Lower highs and lower lows

---

## ➖ Sideways Trend

A sideways trend occurs when prices move within a narrow range without a clear upward or downward direction.

This usually indicates market consolidation.

---

# ✔ Trend Line Validation

A trend line becomes more reliable when:

* It touches multiple price points.
* Price respects the line without breaking it.
* The trend continues for a significant duration.
* Trading volume supports the movement.

The more confirmation points a trend line has, the stronger its significance.
