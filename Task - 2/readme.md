## Technical Indicators Researched

### Moving Average (MA)

Used to identify overall market trends by smoothing price fluctuations.

---

### Exponential Moving Average (EMA)

Gives more importance to recent prices and reacts faster to market movements.

---

### Relative Strength Index (RSI)

Measures the speed and magnitude of price movements.

Typically:

* RSI > 70 → Overbought
* RSI < 30 → Oversold

---

### MACD

Moving Average Convergence Divergence helps identify trend changes using moving averages.

---

### Bollinger Bands

Used to measure market volatility and identify potential breakout points.

---

### Volume

Represents the number of shares traded during a specific period.

High trading volume generally indicates stronger market participation.

---

### Support and Resistance

Support is the price level where buying pressure increases.

Resistance is the price level where selling pressure increases.
